About the Author

Weaving Worlds from Shadow

Leona Kincaid writes at the intersection of beauty and dread — dark fantasy novels that pull readers into worlds where magic is dangerous, love is costly, and the line between monster and hero dissolves in the dark.

Her work draws from folklore, mythology, and the gothic literary tradition, crafting stories that linger long after the final page. She believes the best fantasy holds a mirror to the human condition — and sometimes the reflection is not what we hoped to see.

When not writing, Leona walks her dogs at midnight through empty streets, researches the architecture of pain and the human condition, and paints in her studio. Stories are life — she makes space for the ones that hurt to tell, and hurt more to keep silent.
